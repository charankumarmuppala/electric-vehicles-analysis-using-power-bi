
# ⚡ Electric Vehicle (EV) Sales Dashboard – India

## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** that analyzes Electric Vehicle (EV) sales in India. It covers multiple dimensions such as states, manufacturers, fiscal years, quarters, and Year-over-Year (YoY) growth.

The dashboard helps users understand market trends, identify top-performing regions and companies, and explore the growth of EV adoption in India.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze total EV sales across India
* Identify top EV manufacturers and states
* Track Year-over-Year (YoY) growth
* Compare EV market share by makers
* Provide data-driven insights using interactive visuals

---

## 📌 Key Insights

* **Total EV Sales:** 2M+
* **EV Market Share:** 3.61%
* **YoY Growth:** 97.43%
* **Top State:** Maharashtra
* **Top Maker:** OLA Electric

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

* 📍 EV Sales by State
* 🏭 EV Sales by Manufacturer
* 📅 EV Sales by Fiscal Year & Quarter
* 📊 YoY Growth Analysis
* 🗺️ Interactive India Map Visualization
* 🧩 Treemap for sales distribution by makers
* 🔄 Toggle between 2-Wheelers & 4-Wheelers
